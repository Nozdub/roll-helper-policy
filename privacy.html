Privacy Policy

Roll Helper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app stores a record of your previous dice rolls locally on your device. This data is used solely to provide you with a roll history inside the app. We do not collect this data, have no access to it, and it never leaves your device.

The app does not use external servers, analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. It also does not require you to log in or provide any personal information.
